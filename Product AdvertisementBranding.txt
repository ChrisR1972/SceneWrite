Product Advertisement/Branding

Product Name
Acme Smart Electric Bike

What is the product
The ACME Smart Electric Bike is a sleek, AI-powered urban e-bike designed to make city commuting faster, smoother, and more efficient. With adaptive pedal assist, a long-range battery, and an integrated smart display, it transforms everyday travel into an effortless, connected ride.

Core Benefit/Promise
Smart pedal assist adapts automatically, Long-range battery for daily commuting, Integrated digital display with ride stats, Eco-friendly and cost-efficient, Sleek, modern design

Target Audience
The target audience for the ACME Smart Electric Bike is urban professionals and commuters aged 22–45 who want a faster, more efficient alternative to cars and public transport. They value smart technology, sustainability, modern design, and saving time during their daily commute.

Brand Personality
Innovative, intelligent, urban, confident, forward-thinking, eco-conscious, energetic, modern, empowering, sleek.

Mandatory Inclusions
ACME Smart Electric Bike (full product name), clear hero shot of the bike, smart pedal assist feature, integrated digital display visible, long-range battery mention, urban commuting context, sustainability message, brand logo, tagline “Ride Smarter”, strong closing call-to-action.
